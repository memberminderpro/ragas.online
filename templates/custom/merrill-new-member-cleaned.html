New Member Information

Thank you for your interest in joining Rotary, an organization committed to making the world a better place by putting service above self. We are thrilled to connect with individuals like you who share our values and vision for community and global improvement.

This form is not an application but a way for us to connect and explore the possibilities of your involvement in Rotary. We welcome everyone who wishes to join us in our mission, and we look forward to the opportunity to work together for a better world.

Why Do You Want to Join Rotary?

Your privacy is important to Rotary International and The Rotary Foundation (collectively, "Rotary") and the personal data you share with Rotary will only be used for official Rotary business. This means that the personal data you provide will primarily be used for your potential and (if you are invited to join a Rotary club) active membership in a Rotary club, which includes inviting you to participate in other membership opportunities, membership data reporting purposes, financial processing, supporting The Rotary Foundation, facilitating event planning, communicating key organizational messages and responding to your inquiries. The personal data you provide will be shared with Rotary club and district officers and their delegates; if you are in Great Britain or Ireland, your information may be shared with Rotary International in Great Britain and Ireland (RIBI). Personal data collected on this form is subject to Rotary's Privacy Policy.
